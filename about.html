About Omnivara

Omnivara develops AI systems that accelerate scientific discovery, engineering design, and computational research.

Our long-term vision is to build intelligent research systems capable of assisting scientists and engineers in solving complex problems across physics, photonics, materials science, and computational research.

Omnivara is currently focused on AI for scientific discovery, photonics and metasurface intelligence, autonomous research workflows, and engineering design automation.
